About Us

We are committed to creating and sustaining the conditions that enable all Goodluck Jonathan University students to
experience an unparalleled educational journey that is intellectually, socially, and personally transformative. When you attend Goodluck Jonathan University, you become a part of the rich history of the nation’s new generation
institution of higher learning. Founded in 2022, Goodluck Jonathan University sets to move vividly with life changes
over the centuries to come, but always serving as a haven for the world’s most ambitious scholars and leaders.

We Are Professional

Goodluck Jonathan University (GJU) is a university with its largest campus in Oshikango,
the picturesque diplomatic hub of the border city in the Northern Region of Namibia So We’re proud to stand among the world’s leading research universities.
